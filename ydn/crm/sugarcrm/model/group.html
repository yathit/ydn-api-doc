Class ydn.crm.sugarcrm.model.Group

SugarCRM record fields are organized into group. For example group `name` has field of `first_name`, `last_name`, `salutation` and `full_name` fields. Some field does not have group, such as `id`. They are group into `""` (empty string) group name.
